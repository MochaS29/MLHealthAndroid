Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products

When will the apps be available?

We're launching our beta program in Q2 2025, with full public release scheduled for Q3 2025. Join our launch list to get early access!

How do you protect user privacy?

Privacy is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption, never sell user data, and all AI processing happens on-device when possible. You maintain complete control over your data with easy export and deletion options.

Will there be a free version?

Yes! Each app offers a free trial period so you can experience the full features before subscribing. After the trial, premium subscriptions will unlock continued access to advanced analytics, unlimited storage, and priority support.

Can I use multiple apps together?

Absolutely! Our apps are designed to work seamlessly together. Data can be shared between apps (with your permission) to provide comprehensive insights across all aspects of your wellness journey.

What platforms will be supported?

We're launching with iOS and Android mobile apps, plus a full web application. Desktop apps for Windows and Mac are planned for late 2025.
